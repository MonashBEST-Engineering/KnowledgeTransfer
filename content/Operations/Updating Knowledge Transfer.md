Start all docs with the following:
> ---
> title: [enter your title here]
> tags: [enter as many relevant words or phrases, separated by commas]
> ---
MUST DO THIS:
> **Last Revision: [enter last revision date]**
> ** means bold

> # [enter headings here]
# create headings

> [enter text here]	

> [creates a note]

> - this makes a dot point
For hyperlinks do square brackets followed by round brackets:
> [hyperlink name](put actual link here)
For images:
![](media/[name of image])
*use single asterisk for italicisation 
For Subheadings use ##
Don’t bold titles because it is auto bolded 
Check orange dot and run to make it turn into green tick
